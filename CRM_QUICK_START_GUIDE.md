# CRM Quick Start Guide

## Welcome to the Enhanced CRM! 🎉

Your CRM has been completely redesigned to make managing leads and opportunities easier, faster, and more enjoyable.

---

## 🚀 Getting Started (30 seconds)

### 1. Access the CRM
- Click "CRM" in the sidebar
- You'll see your leads or opportunities in beautiful cards

### 2. Switch Between Leads & Opportunities
- Click the "Leads" or "Opportunities" tabs at the top

### 3. Choose Your View
Click the view icons (top right):
- **Grid** 📊 - Cards in a grid (default)
- **List** 📋 - Cards in a list
- **Kanban** 🎯 - Board view for opportunities
- **Analytics** 📈 - Charts and insights

---

## 💡 Common Tasks (Step-by-Step)

### Create a New Lead
1. Click the blue **"+ New Lead"** button
2. Fill in the company and contact details
3. Click **"Save"**
✅ Done! Your lead is created and assigned to you

### Find a Specific Lead
1. Type in the search box at the top
2. Results appear instantly as you type
✅ No need to click search!

### Filter Leads by Criteria
1. Click the **"Filters"** button
2. The advanced filters expand
3. Choose your criteria (status, source, value, etc.)
4. Results update automatically
5. Click **"Clear All Filters"** to reset

### Convert a Lead to Customer
1. Find the lead card
2. Hover over it
3. Click the **"Convert"** button that appears
4. Confirm the conversion
✅ Customer created automatically!

### Log an Activity
1. Find the lead or opportunity card
2. Hover over it
3. Click the **"Log Activity"** button
4. Fill in what you did
5. Click **"Save"**
✅ Activity logged!

### Edit a Lead or Opportunity
1. Hover over the card
2. Click the three dots **"..."** (more menu)
3. Click **"Edit"**
4. Make your changes
5. Click **"Save"**

### Mark an Opportunity as Won
1. Find the opportunity card
2. Click the three dots **"..."**
3. Click **"Mark as Won"**
✅ Congratulations on the win! 🎉

### Delete a Record
1. Hover over the card
2. Click the three dots **"..."**
3. Click **"Delete"**
4. Confirm deletion
⚠️ Be careful - this cannot be undone!

## 🔍 Advanced Filtering

### Available Filters

**For Leads:**
- 🔍 Search (company, contact, email, phone)
- 📊 Status (new, contacted, qualified, etc.)
- 📌 Source (website, referral, email, etc.)
- 👤 Assigned To (any team member)
- ⭐ Lead Score Range (0-100)
- 💰 Value Range (min-max SAR)
- 📅 Date Range (expected close date)

**For Opportunities:**
- 🔍 Search (name, customer)
- 📊 Stage (creating proposition, accepted, etc.)
- 👤 Assigned To (any team member)
- 💰 Value Range (min-max SAR)
- 📅 Date Range (expected close date)

### Combining Filters
You can use multiple filters at once!

**Example:**
- Status: Qualified
- Score: 80-100
- Value: 100,000+ SAR
= High-value qualified leads

## 🎨 View Modes Explained

### Grid View (Default)
- Cards arranged in columns
- Best for quick scanning
- Shows 1-3 columns based on screen size

**Best for:** Getting an overview, scanning multiple records

### List View
- Cards in a vertical list
- More space per card
- Better for detailed review

**Best for:** Reading details, focused review

### Kanban View (Opportunities Only)
- Drag-and-drop board
- Columns for each stage
- Visual pipeline management

**Best for:** Managing deals through stages, visual workflow

### Analytics View
- Charts and graphs
- Performance metrics
- Trend analysis

**Best for:** Understanding performance, making decisions

## ⚡ Import/Export (Managers & Admins Only)

### Importing Records
1. Click the **"Import"** button
2. Select your Excel/CSV file
3. Records are imported automatically
4. You'll see a success message with count

### Exporting Records
1. Click the **"Export"** button
2. Excel file downloads automatically
3. All current records (or filtered) are exported

### Downloading Template
1. Click the **"Template"** button
2. Empty template downloads
3. Fill it in and import

## 🆘 Common Issues & Solutions

### "No leads found"
**Solution:** Check your filters. Click "Clear All Filters" to reset.

### Cards are loading forever
**Solution:** Refresh the page. Check your internet connection.

### Can't see import/export buttons
**Solution:** These are only available for Managers and Admins.

### Quick actions not appearing
**Solution:** Make sure you're hovering over the card. Wait a moment for them to fade in.

### Search not working
**Solution:** Clear filters first, then try searching again.
