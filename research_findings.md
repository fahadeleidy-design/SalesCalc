# Modern CPQ Features Research Findings

## Top 10 Features Every CPQ Application Should Offer (2025)

Source: https://www.cincom.com/blog/cpq/top-features-every-cpq-application-should-offer/

### 1. Dynamic Product Configuration
- Rules-based engine for complex configurations
- Handles ETO (Engineer-to-Order) and CTO (Configure-to-Order)
- Automates dependencies, constraints, and compatibility checks
- Reduces quoting errors and accelerates response times

### 2. Guided Selling
- Smart recommendations based on requirements and preferences
- Interactive interface that reduces product options dynamically
- Reduces dependency on senior staff
- Improves customer experience and conversion rates

### 3. Automated Pricing & Discounting
- Complex pricing management (volume-based, contract-based, promotional)
- Enforces pricing standards with negotiation flexibility
- Margin analysis and real-time cost calculations
- Automated approval workflows for special pricing

### 4. Seamless ERP & CRM Integration
- Real-time data transfer across departments
- Access to customer history and preferences
- Accurate inventory availability and production timelines
- CAD integration for production-ready designs

### 5. Quote & Proposal Automation
- Professional, error-free document generation
- Customized quotes with branding and specifications
- Multi-page proposals with images and ROI analysis
- Easy revisions and fast turnaround

### 6. Approval Workflow Management
- Automated routing based on business rules
- Multi-tier approval processes
- Notifications and escalation features
- Prevents bottlenecks and unapproved discounts

### 7. Visualization & 3D Configuration
- 2D/3D renderings, AR, and VR features
- Real-time product representations
- CAD integrations for technical drawings
- Reduces errors from misinterpretations

### 8. Multi-channel & Multi-language Support
- Support for direct sales, partners, e-commerce, self-service
- Multi-currency and multi-language capabilities
- Region-specific pricing and tax calculations
- Consistent experience across all touchpoints

### 9. Analytics & Reporting
- Insights into pricing patterns and sales performance
- Track quote conversion rates, deal sizes, approval times
- AI-powered forecasting and optimization
- Customizable dashboards and reports

### 10. Scalability & Cloud Deployment
- Supports growing number of quotes, products, users
- Cloud-based for flexibility and automatic updates
- Remote access and easy integration
- Future-proof for market shifts and expansion

## Key Statistics
- Organizations using CPQ software see **17% higher lead conversion rate** than non-CPQ users
- CPQ streamlines sales process by automating pricing, approvals, and quote generation
